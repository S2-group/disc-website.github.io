## About the Event
In close collaboration with DANW, DiSC is organizing a tutorial on *Digital Architecture in the Age of GenAI*.

### Target Audience
**Practitioners**
- Architecting agentic AI systems
- Exploring GenAI for software architecture practices
- Building AI-enabled software systems

**Researchers**
- Working at the intersection of software architecture (SA) and AI
- Improving architecting practices for AI-enabled systems
- Adopting LLMs for SA research

## Agenda (13:30–18:30)

**13:30–14:00 — Machine Learning (ML) and Software Architecture**
- Synchronizing terminology
- Basics of ML for SA and SA for ML

**14:00–15:00 — LLMs as an Architect Assistant**
- Evaluating architectural decisions generated by LLMs
- When and how to integrate LLMs into architecting practices
- Critical assessment of the role of LLMs in software architecture
- **Takeaway:** Understanding how LLMs can act as co-pilots in architecture-related tasks

**15:00–15:30 — Break**

**15:30–16:30 — LLMs for Architecting (cont.) + Hands-on session**

**16:30–17:30 — Agentic AI and Sustainability**
- Architecting LLM agents and multi-agent systems
- Energy and sustainability implications
- **Takeaway:** AI-enabled architecture as a product
- **Takeaway:** Key considerations for architecting sustainable LLM-enabled systems

**17:30–18:30 — Networking Drinks**
